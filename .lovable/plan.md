

# Landing Pages — Updated Plan

Everything from the previously approved plan remains the same, with this one correction:

## Changed: "How It Works" Section

**Old (inaccurate):**
3 steps: Register Your Lab → Configure Parameters → Start Analyzing

**New (matches real workflow):**
4 steps reflecting the actual LIMS lifecycle:

| Step | Title | Description |
|------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | **Register Your Lab** | Create your organization, pick your industry suite, and invite your team |
| 2 | **Configure Your Tests** | Set up parameters, methods, matrices, and validation rules |
| 3 | **Create Projects & Log Samples** | Register client projects, generate sample IDs, and assign tests |
| 4 | **Review, Approve & Report** | Enter results, run QA validation, and generate Certificates of Analysis |

Each step will have an icon, heading, short description, and a connecting arrow/line between them. This accurately represents the 5-stage workflow (Configuration → Project Setup → Sample Registration → Results Entry → Approval) condensed into 4 user-friendly marketing steps.

All other plan details (pages, components, routing, design) remain unchanged from the approved plan.
